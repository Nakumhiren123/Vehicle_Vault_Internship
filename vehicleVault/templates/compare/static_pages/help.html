Frequently Asked Questions
Why am I not receiving my OTP email?▾
Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's not there, make sure the email address on your account is correct. OTPs expire after 5 minutes — if yours expired, click "Resend OTP" on the login screen. If the issue persists, contact us at hello@vehiclevault.com.
How do I reset my password?▾
Go to your Profile page and click "Reset Password" in the Account Details section. You'll need to enter your current password and then choose a new one. Your new password must be at least 8 characters long.
How do I compare two vehicles?▾
Navigate to the vehicle list from your dashboard, select any car, and use the "Compare" button. You can compare two vehicles side-by-side across all specs including fuel type, mileage, transmission, price, and launch year.
Can I edit or delete a review I submitted?▾
Yes. Go to your Profile → My Reviews to see all your submitted reviews. From there you can edit the rating or comment, or delete the review entirely. Deletions are permanent.
How do I delete my account?▾
To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us at privacy@vehiclevault.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 7 business days.
Is VehicleVault free to use?▾
Yes, VehicleVault is completely free for all registered users. Browse vehicles, compare specs, and submit reviews at no cost. We have no plans to introduce paid tiers for core features.
