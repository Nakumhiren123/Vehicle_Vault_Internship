01 —
Information We Collect

When you register on VehicleVault, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name and email address
  • Gender and account role (user or admin)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — never in plain text externally)
  • Account creation date and last activity timestamp

We also automatically collect usage data such as vehicles viewed, comparisons made, reviews submitted, and general interaction logs to improve your experience.

02 —
How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power your VehicleVault experience:

  • Authenticating your account and keeping it secure with OTP login
  • Displaying your activity stats and personalised dashboard
  • Sending transactional emails (OTPs, welcome messages, password resets)
  • Improving our platform based on anonymised usage patterns
🔒 We never use your data for targeted advertising or sell it to third parties under any circumstances.
03 —
Data Sharing

VehicleVault does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated data (e.g. "most compared cars this month") publicly, but this data is never traceable to individual users.

We use Django's email infrastructure to send transactional emails. Email delivery providers may process your email address in transit — they are contractually prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

04 —
Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your account:

  • Passwords are hashed using Django's PBKDF2-SHA256 algorithm
  • Login requires OTP verification sent to your registered email
  • All sessions use CSRF protection and signed cookies
  • HTTPS is enforced on all pages in production

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is 100% immune. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your email account secure.

05 —
Your Rights

You have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect or outdated information via your profile
  • Deletion: Request complete removal of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vehiclevault.com. We will respond within 7 business days.
